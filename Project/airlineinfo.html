Our Origins

Homestead Air was founded out of an attempt to experience first-class for economy pricing. Expensive airlines often charge tons of money for a flying experience that was mediocre at best, whereas discount airlines offer cramped spaces, terrible customer service, lost baggage, and worst of all, hidden fees.

Our Mission

With Homestead Air, you will never have to worry about losing baggage, sitting in constricted seating, and overall abysmal customer service, both in flight and out of flight. Additionally, we prioritize transparency, ensuring that no hidden fees will come up ruining your vacation budget.
