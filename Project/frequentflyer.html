Frequent Flyer Information

Here at CAirlines, we value your loyalty. Our Frequent Flyer Program reflects this, so opposed to other airlines, we offer miles that stick with you for life, instead of disappearing after one year. We've sorted our flyers into different tiers, but compared to other airlines, we trust that you will stick with our amazing service, so our mile requirements are much lower than regular airlines. Our four tiers are: - Silver: Earned by customers with more than 25,000 miles. - Gold: Earned by customers with more than 40,000 miles. - Platinum: Earned by customers with more than 75,000 miles. - Premiere: Earned by customers with more than 100,00 miles. At CAirlines, no matter what tier you are placed in, numerous benefits await you, both in and out of the aircraft. The benefits offered are as follows: - Silver: Free, unlimited non-alcoholic drinks and snacks offered during flight and before boarding. - Gold: Silver's perks, with alcoholic drinks offered during flight and before boarding, earlier boarding than Silver. - Platinum: Front seating offered, more leg space, earlier boarding than Gold. - Premiere: Free upgrades to upper class seating if available, no flight change fee. content: we offer lifetime miles unlike other airlines things to talk about: tiered by: silver is less than 25000 miles gold is less than 40000 miles platinum is less than 75000 miles premiere is less than 100000 miles benefits: silver: free non alcoholic drinks and snacks during Flight gold: alcoholic drinks provided during flight platinum: preferred seating - sit in the front, more leg space, early boarding premiere: no airline change fee, free upgrades(if available first class tickets given to premiere members),
